I'm a junior studying Computer Science and Sociology at the University of Washington, focusing on Human-AI Interaction. I work with [Prof. Jacob Wobbrock](https://faculty.washington.edu/wobbrock/) at [UW’s ACE Lab](https://depts.washington.edu/acelab/), and previously worked with [Prof. Xin Yi](https://www.insc.tsinghua.edu.cn/inscen/info/1255/1114.htm) at [Tsinghua’s INSC](https://www.insc.tsinghua.edu.cn/inscen/).

My work focuses on building AI-driven interactive systems that are aligned with human intent and augment human capability. I combine HCI research with full-stack engineering to turn design concepts into real-world products—creating AI systems that people can understand, trust, and meaningfully benefit from.

I grew up in Shanghai, China. When I'm not researching, you can find me sailing, traveling, or behind a camera. Always happy to chat—feel free to reach out!
